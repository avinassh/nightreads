What are Nightreads?

Every night I will send an email with a link to an interesting read. The links will never have news items. There are plenty of places where you can get latest tech news. Choose your interests in the above box and I will send the links accordingly. And select all to receive every email.

You can suggest me interesting links via Twitter, email or join our Devup Slack Team. I plan to build a webinterface to manage all this in future.

Nightreads was originally started by our member @gnu in Devup Slack.
