What is Nightreads?

Every night we will email a link to an interesting read. The links will never contain news or ads of any sort. There are plenty of places where you can find your fix of latest tech news. Select your interests in the box above and we will send the links based on your choices. Select all to receive email on all topics.

You can suggest us interesting links via Twitter, Email or join our Devup Slack Team. We plan to build a web interface to manage this in future.

Nightreads was originally started by our beloved member @gnu in Devup Slack.
